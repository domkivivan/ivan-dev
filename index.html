Oasis
Lounge

A complete digital product for a premium hookah bar — guest app, real-time staff panel, loyalty engine and booking system, all shipped as a single Telegram Mini App.

  • Table booking in 3 steps — interactive floor map, time slots, conflict protection
  • 5-tier loyalty system: Bronze → VIP, points catalogue, transaction history
  • Real-time guest–staff chat via Socket.IO
  • Staff panel — booking lifecycle, table grid, content management, settings
  • Telegram Bot push notifications: booking status, points earned, news & promos
